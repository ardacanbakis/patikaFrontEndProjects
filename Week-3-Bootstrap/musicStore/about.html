About Us

Welcome to Music Store, your number one source for all things musical. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of instruments and accessories, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by Arda Canbakis, Music Store has come a long way from its beginnings. When Arda first started out, his passion for music drove him to start his own business, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the musical instruments industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
